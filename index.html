    I see a huge perspective in the computer field. Technology keeps growing, with robotics and AI taking the spotlight in recent years. But I think we are far from the end, and the demand for motivated computer engineering will only continue to grow. This cannot be said about many other jobs with uncertain future. Robots will replace humans in certain professions, However, this won't be true for the people who create and program these robots. And that's the side of the barricade where I'd like to stand once I finish the university. The degree in computer engineering is an important step on this journey.
